About Us

Founded in 2017 in Mombasa Kenya, Flavours Fusion Restaurant has been serving the community with the finest Swahili & Arab cuisine. Our chef, Faud Muhamud, has over 20 years of experience in the culinary arts and is dedicated to providing an exceptional dining experience.

We pride ourselves on using only the freshest local ingredients combined with authentic spices and traditional cooking methods to create dishes that tell a story of our rich coastal heritage.
